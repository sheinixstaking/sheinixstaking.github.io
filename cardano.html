Summary

You can earn ~5% annual by staking in Cardano. The minimun to start staking is 10 ADAs. When you start staking in Cardano 2 ADAs will be taken from your wallet by the Cardano Protocol as a deposit on the network. You will get back those 2 deposited ADA once you deregister from staking. In Cardano your money is NOT sent to us, you will have access to your funds at any time since they will always be in your wallet. There is no locked time for withdrawals. You will need to wait at least 20 days to see your first reward. The more ADA you delegate to our pool the more rewards you will get when we mint blocks. You can use Yoroi Wallet, Daedalus Wallet, CCWallet.io to start staking with us.
